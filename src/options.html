CopyCat needs following permissions:
  • tabs - Retrieve URL and Title information
  • clipboardWrite - After retrieve URL and Title, it writes into your clipboard
  • storage - this is used to store configuration settings
  • notifications - Notification silently pops when you use shortcut keys
  • contextMenus - Right Click Menu
